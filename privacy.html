("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our plumbing request service.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our website, you agree to be bound by the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the practices described in this policy, please do not use our website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide To Us

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you fill out contact forms, request plumbing service information, subscribe to communications, communicate with us, participate in surveys, or request quotes or provider contact. This may include your name, email address, phone number, mailing or service address, service category, project details, urgency level, photos or notes you choose to provide, and any other information you submit.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing behavior, including IP address and approximate location data, browser type and version, operating system, pages visited, time spent on pages, referring website addresses, and device information.

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activities. For more details, please see our Cookie Policy.
